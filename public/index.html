❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How are Powerball numbers drawn?

Numbers are drawn using two mechanical drums. One drum contains 69 white balls, and the other contains 26 red Powerballs. This physical randomness ensures total transparency.

Can AI predict the next winning numbers?

No system can predict a random event with 100% certainty. However, AI can help eliminate 'unlikely' patterns that rarely appear in history, narrowing down the field based on probability.

What is the "Power Play" multiplier?

For an extra $1, you can multiply your non-jackpot winnings by 2, 3, 4, 5, or even 10 times. It’s a high-impact way to boost second-tier prizes.

Is there a deadline to claim a jackpot?

Yes. Claim periods vary by state, ranging from 90 days to 1 year. Always check the back of your ticket or the official state lottery website for exact deadlines.
